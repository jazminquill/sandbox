# Fabulous App — Content Creation Project

## About This Project
This repo supports content creation for **The Fabulous** (thefabulous.co), a wellness and habit-building app developed from Duke University's Behavioral Economics Lab. The app helps 37M+ users build healthy habits through behavioral science-backed journeys, coaching, and educational content.

## The Fabulous App — Content Types (Detailed)

### 1. Journeys
- Multi-week structured programs centered on themes (sleep, focus, energy, weight loss, anxiety)
- Broken into chapters with progressive habit-building
- Only one Journey active at a time per user
- Each Journey contains letters, coaching, and actions

### 2. Letters
- Motivational/educational messages delivered within Journeys
- Filled with insights, encouragement, and behavioral science
- Over 500 coaching letters exist in the app
- Should be engaging but not overly long
- Written in a warm, personal, story-driven tone

### 3. Multi-Step Letters
- Letters that span multiple screens/steps
- Guide users through a progression of ideas or actions
- See `Fabulous Multi-Step Letters Guidelines.pdf` for structure

### 4. Mini-Games
- Short interactive/gamified experiences within the app
- Used for engagement, education, and behavior reinforcement
- See `Fabulous Mini Game Structures copy.pdf` for formats and rules

### 5. Paged Content
- Content displayed in a paginated/scrollable format
- See `Fabulous Paged Content.pdf` for layout guidelines

### 6. Coaching Sessions
- 2-3 minute pre-recorded motivational audio talks
- Types: Daily, Focus, Nightly, and Coaching Series
- Topics include: burnout, self-care, emotional regulation, grief, habit-building, time management, anxiety, relationships
- Shape Coaching Series focuses on health, body respect, mindful eating

### 7. Routine Rituals
- Morning, Afternoon, and Evening ritual blocks
- Start with small keystone habits (e.g., drinking water)
- Gradually layer in new habits as confidence builds

### 8. Challenges
- Time-limited engagement activities for users

### 9. Quizzes
- Interactive quiz-based content for education and engagement

### 10. Guides
- Educational guide content on wellness topics

### 11. Zen Meditations
- Meditation scripts and guided relaxation content

### 12. Hypnosis
- Hypnosis/deep relaxation scripts

### 13. Ambiance & BGM
- Ambient sound design and background music for immersive experiences

### 14. Summaries
- Recap/summary content for sessions and journeys

## Writing Style & Tone
- **Science-backed:** Content is rooted in behavioral economics and psychology
- **Story-driven:** Journeys are framed as chapters in the user's personal story
- **Warm and personal:** Like a supportive coach or wise friend
- **Encouraging, not preachy:** Motivate through positive framing
- **Concise:** Respect the user's time; avoid overly long blocks of text
- **Accessible:** Write for broad health literacy levels; avoid jargon
- **Sensitive language:** Avoid terms that could feel demeaning (e.g., careful with words like "obesity," "infirmity")

## Target Audience
- Adults looking to build healthier habits
- Especially effective for beginners to structured self-improvement
- People who feel overwhelmed by drastic lifestyle changes
- Users seeking gradual, science-backed behavior change

## App Design Context
- Colorful, illustration-rich visual design
- Notifications designed as friendly moral support (not spam)
- Engagement triggers: song, illustration, countdown clock
- Onboarding quiz covers sleep, focus, health feelings, and interest areas
